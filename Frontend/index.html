About EV_aluate

Architecture, methodology, and use-case documentation for the EV Intelligence Platform

The Platform

EV_aluate is a dual-model machine learning platform engineered to quantify two critical dimensions of electric vehicle performance: environmental sustainability through CO₂ savings prediction, and technological advancement through a composite Innovation Score.

Built on XGBoost and Linear Regression architectures, both models exceed 99% predictive accuracy on a curated dataset of 360 European electric vehicles sourced from the EV Database.

Methodology

Data Pipeline

01

Data Collection

360 EV records sourced from ev-database.org with 9 raw features per vehicle

02

Target Engineering

CO₂ savings formula: (range × 0.12) − (efficiency × range / 1000 × 0.4)

03

Innovation Scoring

Composite index = 40% Tech Edge + 40% Energy Intelligence + 20% User Value

04

Outlier Removal

IQR-based outlier detection with 1.5× fence — ensures robust generalization

05

Feature Scaling

Min-Max normalization to [0, 1] range for consistent model behavior

06

Feature Selection

Pearson correlation analysis selecting optimal 5-6 feature subset

07

Train-Test Split

80% training / 20% testing with stratified sampling for balanced evaluation

08

Hyperparameter Tuning

GridSearchCV optimization for XGBoost — n_estimators, max_depth, learning_rate

09

Cross-Validation

5-fold CV delivering R² = 0.9938 (CO₂) and 0.9924 (Innovation)
